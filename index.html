Who Are WE

We are the best amoungst the rest

Founded in 2008, we are a proudly Zimbabwean planning consultancy and construction firm dedicated to building a better Zimbabwe—true to the spirit of “Nyika inovakwa nevene vayo.”
Our integrated services span Town Planning, Urban Design, Project Coordination, and Construction, allowing us to deliver end-to-end solutions from concept to completion. With projects completed across the country, we bring a wealth of experience and creativity to every development—residential, commercial, or industrial.
We believe in collaboration—working with clients, communities, and government to create sustainable, livable spaces that meet today’s needs and serve future generations. With every project, we’re not just building structures—we’re building a nation
